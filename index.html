Research

I'm interested in temporal interpretation of modal expressions and modal interpretation of temporal expressions. I have worked on the question why certain readings of modal auxiliaries obligatorily induce a temporally forward shifted reading of the prejacent proposition. And I'm trying to find out more about why 'distance from reality' meanings are often expressed using past tense morphology across languages.

Activities

BerlinBrnoVienna student workshop

Together with David Müller, we organise the student workshop BerlinBrnoVienna in Brno, Czech Republic.

Why the name? Previously this workshop aimed to bring together specifically the students of Humbold University in Berlin, University of Vienna and Masaryk University Brno. In recent years, the workshop grew more and more international, featuring talks by students from various European or American universities.

(Previous co-organizers: Anne-Li Demonie, Karolin Kaiser, Luisa Kalvelage, Veronika Kloudová, Valerie Wurm, Paul Zodl)
